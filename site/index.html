How it works

A challenge is just a public post. Every reply carries one move, and the thread itself becomes the board.

1

Challenge

Choose a game, make your opening move, and post it to your rival. It reads as a normal post — anyone can see it.

2

They reply

Your rival installs Gage, sees the live board in the thread, and replies with their move. Back and forth, one move per reply.

3

The thread is the game

No server keeps score. Gage reads the reply chain and rebuilds the position — the public thread is the record of play.
