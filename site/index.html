Both players need the Gage extension

Gage plays inside the X page itself — it reads the thread, draws a live board, and fills your reply. That means you and your rival both need the extension installed to play a duel out. It isn’t on the Chrome Web Store yet, so load it unpacked (desktop Chrome):

  1. Download or clone the Gage repository to your machine.
  2. Open chrome://extensions and switch on Developer mode (top-right).
  3. Click Load unpacked and select the repository folder.
  4. Open x.com — the Gage board panel appears bottom-right. You’re armed.

The challenge you cast from this page is a normal X post: your rival sees it even without the extension. But to reply with moves and see the board, they’ll need Gage too — point them here.
